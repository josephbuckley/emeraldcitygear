## What to Watch For

### Can the Rotation Settle Down?

The starting pitching has been inconsistent, to put it charitably. **Logan Gilbert** took the loss in both of his April starts so far. **George Kirby** — who was supposed to be the ace — has struggled to lock down games when it matters, going 0-2 with a loss at Texas. **Emerson Hancock** threw a gem in Game 4 to beat Cleveland, but gave one back in Anaheim.

This Astros series is a gut check for the rotation. Houston isn't the 100-win juggernaut they were a few years ago, but they're not pushovers either. The M's need a couple of quality starts here to give the offense a chance to breathe.

### Julio and the Lineup Need to Wake Up

**Julio Rodríguez** was supposed to enter 2026 as one of the best players in the AL. That's still probably true — but the early returns haven't been explosive. When Julio is locked in, this offense is electric. When he's grinding through a slump, the whole lineup feels flat.

The home crowd at T-Mobile has a way of charging him up. If you're going to a game this weekend, don't sleep on the possibility of watching J-Rod do something that makes you forget the losing streak entirely.
