Shopping for a Mariners fan is either super easy or quietly stressful, depending on how well you know them.

Easy if you know their jersey number preference and size. Stressful if you’re buying for someone whose whole identity is the M’s and you don’t want to show up with a Griffey jersey when they’re a die-hard Cal Raleigh fan.

This guide is here to help. Whether you’re shopping for a birthday, Christmas, Father’s Day, or just “I saw this and thought of you” — here’s what actually makes Mariners fans happy, organized by type of fan and budget.

Best Mariners Jerseys (The Classic Move)#

A Mariners jersey is the gift that never gets old. The question is which one.

The Current Stars: Julio Rodríguez and Cal Raleigh#

If you’re buying for a fan who follows the current roster, two names dominate:

Julio Rodríguez — The face of the franchise, the one the next generation of Mariners fans will tell their kids about. The Navy replica jersey with his name and number is the most popular jersey in the Mariners store right now, and for good reason. It’s clean, it’s iconic, and Julio’s going to be wearing it for years.

Cal Raleigh — Big Dumper has one of the most enthusiastic fan bases on the roster. The “Big Dumper” nickname has taken on a life of its own, and his gear flies off the shelves after every walk-off.

The Retro Pick: Ken Griffey Jr.#

You can’t go wrong with Griffey. The Junior #24 in classic teal is still one of the best-looking baseball jerseys ever made. Timeless.

👉 Shop Mariners Jerseys on Fanatics

Budget tip: Replica jerseys run $120–$150. Authentic jerseys are $200–$350. For most gift recipients, replica is the right call.

Best Mariners Hats#

A hat works when you’re not sure about jersey size, style preference, or budget.

The 59FIFTY Fitted Hat#

New Era 59FIFTY fitted hats in Mariners navy are a staple. The primary navy with the trident logo is the safest bet — it goes with everything.

The ’90s Teal Throwback Hat#

If the person grew up in the Randy Johnson era, the teal throwback cap delivers the nostalgia. The Mariners’ mid-’90s teal and navy has had a full comeback.

City Connect Hat#

The Mariners’ City Connect gear — inspired by Pacific Northwest aesthetics — has been a fan favorite for something a bit different from standard navy.

👉 Shop Mariners Hats on Fanatics

  • Mariners Phone Case — Fanatics and Casetify both have solid options
  • Mariners Socks — Sounds low-key but fans genuinely love good novelty socks
  • Mariners T-Shirt — Look for 47 Brand soft-style tees; avoid the cheap generic ones that pill immediately

Best Mariners Gifts for Kids#

Kids grow fast and Mariners fans train them early.

  • Youth Julio Rodríguez Jersey — He’s the player kids are watching right now
  • Mariners Kids Hat — Works at any budget, easily sized
  • Mariners Backpack — Practical, used constantly, available on Fanatics
  • Youth Baseball Glove — Pair a Rawlings or Wilson glove in navy with a Mariners hat for a full starter-pack gift

Best Gifts for the Obsessed Fan#

For the fan who already has three jerseys:

T-Mobile Park Game Tickets#

The most experiential gift you can give. A home game — especially with good seats — is a memory, not a thing. SeatGeek is the best place to find seats at good prices.

Pro tip: Lower level along first or third base line. Sections 120–135 and 145–160 are the sweet spots.

👉 Find Mariners Tickets on SeatGeek

Personalized Mariners Jersey#

Fanatics lets you put a custom name and number on a Mariners jersey. Turnaround is usually 3–4 weeks, so plan ahead.

Framed Mariners Photo or Print#

A framed photo of Griffey’s swing or a stadium print of T-Mobile Park is the kind of gift that ends up on a wall for years. Great options on Etsy from independent sellers.

Best Mariners Gear for Her#

Most “best Mariners gifts” guides default to men’s jerseys. The women’s gear selection has gotten genuinely good:

  • Women’s Mariners Fitted Cap — Soft fits, adjustable sizing
  • Women’s Mariners T-Shirt — The Fanatics “relaxed fit” line is popular
  • Women’s Replica Jersey — Same players (Julio, Cal, Griffey), cut for a women’s fit
