Seattle Mariners Opening Day 2026: Everything You Need to Be Ready

Opening Day is Thursday, March 26. The Mariners host the Cleveland Guardians at T-Mobile Park, and it’s not just another home opener — 2026 marks the franchise’s 50th anniversary season. There’s extra weight to this one.

Whether you’re going to the park, watching from home, or shopping for gear to mark the occasion, here’s everything you need.

The Game#

Who: Seattle Mariners vs. Cleveland Guardians When: Thursday, March 26, 2026 Where: T-Mobile Park, Seattle

The Mariners open as AL West champs defending their division — and the 50 Seasons patch on their jerseys makes it official that this year means something.

Going to the Game#

Opening Day sells out or gets close. If you haven’t locked in tickets yet, SeatGeek is the best place to find remaining inventory at realistic prices.

A few tips:

  • Get there early. The team is handing out magnetic schedules (presented by Starbucks) to fans on Opening Day. Gates open well before first pitch — don’t show up at game time.
  • Best seats for the money: Lower level along the first or third base lines (sections 120–135 and 145–160). You’re close to the action without paying premium behind home plate.
  • March weather in Seattle: It’s going to be cold and possibly wet. Layer up.

Watching From Home#

Can’t make it to the park? The Mariners’ in-market streaming service, Mariners.TV, is the way to watch every game this season — no cable required. It replaced ROOT Sports for 2026.

  • Price: $99.99 for the full season
  • Works on: Roku, Apple TV, Fire TV, iOS, Android, web browser
  • Blackout rules: In-market only — if you’re in the Seattle viewing area, this is your option
